Classes

Cardio & Fitness

Cardio and fitness training is a great way to improve your health and fitness, results can be seen shortly following our regular plan. This can be discussed with one our trainers to give you your own personalised plan.

Yoga & Pilates

If you're looking to develop core strength and balance, when practised regularly yoga and Pilates are both ideal exercises for this. Yoga uses the body to connect with the mind and the inner self, while pilates uses mindfulness to connect to the inner workings of the body

Functional strength

Functional strength training focuses on exercising several muscles and joints together rather than working a particular muscle or group of muscles independently, resulting in an individual being able to perform daily activities with greater ease.
